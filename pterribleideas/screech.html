What do we do?

Born from the creative chaos of a Seminole State College film class, Screeching Pterodactyl is a Central Florida-based video production company with a wild imagination and a sharp eye for storytelling. Our team specializes in bold, offbeat short films and eye-catching commercials that grab attention and never let go. Whether we're helping brands take flight or crafting quirky tales with a cinematic punch, every project is powered by originality, energy, and just the right amount of prehistoric flair.
